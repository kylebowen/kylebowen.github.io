About

Writing a good Bio is hard. Deciding to work with me is easy. And if there's one thing I love, it's making things easy for other people.

I'm a Web Developer currently based in the the Greater Salt Lake City area. I'm a firm believer in TDD, OOP, SOLID, and all the great acronyms.

I'm more than just an "idea guy" — I deliver value by developing systems and taking things to the next level. I'm much more excited to work with people focused on improving the lives of others, than "solving interesting problems" that no one really needed solved in the first place.

In a former life, I ran a small recording studio and worked as a freelance musician & photographer.

When I'm not solving problems, you can find me:

  • Riding my fixed-gear bicycle.
  • Beatboxing in a cappella groups.
  • Watching Sandi Metz conference talks.
